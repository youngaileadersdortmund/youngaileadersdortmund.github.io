Are you into AI and believe that it should be used for good?
Do you want to connect with other forward-thinkers
and join our collaborative community?
Then apply now and become a Young AI Leader!

Additional Information

Through initiatives like the Young AI Leaders Community, AI for Good ensures that young people have the skills, platforms, and support to lead the way in building a more inclusive and sustainable digital future.
We are proud to be one of 90 hubs around the world, putting AI to good use in the area of Dortmund!
For more information and projects, please refer to the Community Charter and AI for Good Homepage.

We explicitly welcome folks from any background - diversity is a central strength of our community!
This means that anyone aged 18-30 can apply to become a Young AI Leader for up to four years.
Apply via the AI for Good webform - click "APPLY TO JOIN A HUB" and select the Dortmund Hub!

Our Hub is currently led by Raphael Fischer, Nico Koltermann and Bahavathy Kathirgamanathan.
Please help us grow by sharing our Call for Applications and social media posts - thank you!
