Do you want to connect with other forward-thinkers
and join our collaborative community?

Then apply now and become a Young AI Leader!

Additional Information

Through initiatives like the Young AI Leaders Community, AI for Good ensures that young people have the skills, platforms, and support to lead the way in building a more inclusive and sustainable digital future.

Anyone aged 18-30 can apply to take up the voluntary position of a Young AI Leader for up to four years.
Applications are made through the central AI for Good webform, selecting the local Hub.

The Dortmund Hub is currently led by Raphael Fischer - feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
Please help us grow by sharing our Call for Applications and social media posts - thank you!

For more information, please refer to the AI for Good Homepage.
